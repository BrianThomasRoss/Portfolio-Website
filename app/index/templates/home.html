About Me

I love data! While things like neural networks and statistical networks have existed for decades, in some cases centuries, only recently have we been able to unlock the full potential of the incredible amount of data generated by humanity each day. That excites me because it also means that we are entering an era where we finally have the tools to tackle some of our greatest challenges. While I'm no stranger to a notebook, and love to share and discuss recent favorites I've found on arxiv, I take pride and special interest in helping develop ETL pipelines and applications that help take the work of researchers out of Jupyter and into the hands of consumers.
